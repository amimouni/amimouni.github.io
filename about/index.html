A positive social environment

Stimulating

We look for friendly and curious people who enjoy working and want to get better at what they do. Ask questions and seek answers. Tackle new challenges and make new discoveries. Never stop exploring.

Self-directed

We value intrinsic motivation and self-direction, and believe people learn best when they're free to explore their passions and interests.

Thoughtful

The atmosphere here is friendly and intellectual. We have social rules. We like profound respect for each individual as a person and learner. We like asking questions in an environment that is not just productive but also supportive, friendly, and positive.
