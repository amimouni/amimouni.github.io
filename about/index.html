Empowering successful women

We know there are many obstacles women face in order to be successful in tech.
We encourage them to be organised and purposeful in structuring their careers.
We try to open women’s eyes to their own leadership potential, while creating and expanding their networks.

A positive social environment

Stimulating

We are looking for friendly and curious people who enjoy working and want to get better at what they do. People who ask questions and seek answers. People who are comfortable tackling new challenges and making new discoveries. People who never stop exploring.

Focus

It's rare to have such a large, uninterrupted chunk of time to focus on just one thing. At work or not, most of us have multiple responsibilities and priorities to juggle. This is a good place to start a project and test it with the community.

Thoughtful

The atmosphere here is friendly and intellectual. We have social rules. We show respect to each other as people and as learners. We enjoy asking questions in an environment that is not just productive but also supportive, friendly, and positive.

Self-directed

We value intrinsic motivation and self-direction. We believe people learn best when they're free to explore their passions and interests.

Lend a hand

Help out. Leave places better than you found them. Share your unique talents with those around you.

Open the door

Encourage others and invite them to join in. Relish the opportunity to connect with interesting people. Get to know those around you. Dive in and take part in all the community has to offer.

We encourage a healthy work management formula

  • Yoga, jogging, meditation...
  • Work session
  • Daily pairing coaching
  • Start with the hardest or most valuable thing first
  • Timely breaks during the day, we like the 52-17 method
  • Cook lunch or lunch at a restaurant nearby
  • We encourage napping sessions
  • Finish work early
  • Dinner and hanging out with the group
  • A to-do-list prepared the night before
