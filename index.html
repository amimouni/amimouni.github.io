A positive social environment

Stimulating

We look for friendly, self-directed and curious people who enjoy working and want to get better at what they do. Ask questions and seek answers. Tackle new challenges and make new discoveries. Never stop exploring.

Thoughtful

The atmosphere here is friendly and intellectual. We have social rules. We like profound respect for each individual as a person and learner. We like asking questions in an environment that is not just productive but also supportive, friendly, and positive.

Empowering women

We know they are local obstacles women see to success in tech. We encourage women to be deliberate and purposeful in planning their careers. We try to open women’s eyes to leadership possibilities. We create and expand women’s networks.
